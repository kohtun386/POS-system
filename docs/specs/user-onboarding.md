# User Onboarding — User Workflow Guide

## Overview

This guide walks you through everything that happens from the moment you sign up for CoffeeShop POS to your first day using the system. The onboarding process includes account creation, approval by the platform administrator, your first login, and initial shop setup.

## Tier Access

| Tier | Access Level | Notes |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Free | Full onboarding experience | 50 products max, 50 orders/day, basic features only. First tier available after approval. |
| Growth | Same onboarding + expanded features | Unlimited products/orders, raw materials, recipes, receipt printing, cash drawer. |
| Pro | Same onboarding + all features | Everything in Growth plus profit analytics, waste tracking, owner insights, WhatsApp reports. |

All new shops start on the **Free tier** after approval. You can upgrade to Growth or Pro at any time by contacting the platform administrator.

## Step-by-Step Workflow

### Step 1: Sign Up

1. **Go to:** The CoffeeShop POS website
2. **Click:** "Sign Up" or "Create Account"
3. **Fill in the form:**
   - **Email address:** Your shop's email (not a personal email — this becomes the shop's permanent account)
   - **Password:** At least 8 characters
   - **Shop name:** Your coffee shop or tea shop's name (e.g., "Golden Bean Café")
4. **Click:** "Create Account"
5. **System response:** A confirmation email is sent to your email address.

**Important:** Use an email you control permanently. Your shop account is tied to this email. You can change it later, but the initial email should be your shop's official address.

### Step 2: Verify Your Email

1. **Open:** Your email inbox
2. **Find:** The email from CoffeeShop POS (subject: "Verify your email address")
3. **Click:** The verification link in the email
4. **System response:** Your email is verified. You can now sign in.

**If you don't see the email:**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Wait 5 minutes and check again
- Try signing in — the system will offer to resend the verification email

### Step 3: Sign In (Pending Approval)

1. **Go to:** The CoffeeShop POS login page
2. **Enter:** Your email and password
3. **Click:** "Sign In"
4. **System response:** You see a screen that says:

> **Your shop is pending approval**
>
> Thank you for signing up! Your account is being reviewed by the platform administrator. This usually takes 1-2 business days.
>
> You will receive an email notification once your account is approved.
>
> If you have questions, contact support at Gmail - kohtunhtun386@gmail.com, Telegram - @smithkht13.

5. **What you can do now:** Nothing — wait for approval. You cannot access the POS, add products, or do anything until approved.

**Why approval is required:** Every shop is manually reviewed to prevent abuse and ensure the platform stays focused on coffee and tea shops. This is a one-time process.

### Step 4: Approval (Platform Administrator Action)

While you wait, here is what happens behind the scenes:

1. The platform administrator (Ko Htun) sees your signup in the admin dashboard
2. They review: your shop name, email, and that you are a legitimate coffee/tea shop
3. They decide to **Approve** or **Reject**

**If approved:**
- Your account is activated
- Your shop is set to the Free tier
- You receive an email: *"Your shop has been approved! Sign in to get started."*

**If rejected:**
- You receive an email with the reason (e.g., "We currently only support coffee and tea shops")
- Your account remains inactive
- You can contact support to appeal or clarify

### Step 5: First Login (After Approval)

The first time you sign in after approval, you see a guided setup tour.

#### Welcome Screen

> **Welcome to CoffeeShop POS!**
>
> Let's set up your shop in a few quick steps.

The tour walks you through:

#### Step 5a: Shop Settings

1. **Navigate to:** Settings (automatically opened by the tour)
2. **Fill in your shop details:**
   - **Shop Name:** e.g., "Golden Bean Café" (pre-filled from signup)
   - **Address:** Your shop's physical address
   - **Phone:** Your shop's phone number
   - **Email:** Your shop's contact email
   - **Logo:** Upload your shop's logo (appears on receipts)
   - **Currency:** Your primary currency (default: MMK)
   - **Tax Rate:** Your local tax rate (e.g., 5%)
3. **Click:** "Save Settings"

#### Step 5b: Add Your First Product

1. **Navigate to:** Inventory → Products (guided by the tour)
2. **Click:** "Add Product"
3. **Fill in:**
   - **Name:** e.g., "Cappuccino"
   - **Price:** e.g., 2500 (MMK)
   - **Category:** e.g., "Coffee"
   - **Description:** Optional, e.g., "Classic espresso with steamed milk"
   - **Track Inventory:** Toggle ON if you want to track stock
   - **Current Stock:** e.g., 100 (if tracking inventory)
4. **Click:** "Save"
5. **System response:** The product appears in your POS grid.

#### Step 5c: Add Your First Customer (Optional)

1. **Navigate to:** Customers (guided by the tour)
2. **Click:** "Add Customer"
3. **Fill in:**
   - **Name:** e.g., "U Aung Kyaw"
   - **Phone:** e.g., "09123456789"
   - **Email:** Optional
   - **Credit Limit:** Optional (for credit payments)
4. **Click:** "Save"

#### Step 5d: Tour Complete

> **You're all set!**
>
> Head to the POS Terminal to start taking orders. You can always come back to Settings to adjust your shop configuration.

**Click:** "Go to POS" to start using the system.

### Step 7: Upgrade to Growth or Pro

When you are ready for more features, you can upgrade your subscription.

#### How to Upgrade

1. **Contact:** Ko Htun via phone, Viber, or WhatsApp
2. **Discuss:** Which tier you want (Growth or Pro) and for how long
3. **Pay:** Transfer payment via KBZpay, AYApay, UABpay, or MMQR
4. **Send:** Payment receipt to Ko Htun
5. **Wait:** Ko Htun verifies payment and activates your new tier
6. **Receive:** Confirmation email: *"Your subscription has been upgraded to Growth! New features are now available."*

#### What Happens After Upgrade

- **Immediately:** New features become available in your interface
- **No data loss:** All your existing products, sales, and settings are preserved
- **Navigation updates:** New menu items appear (e.g., Raw Materials, Recipes)
- **Feature unlocks:** Receipt printing, raw materials, recipes, reports become accessible

#### Upgrade Prompt Locations

You will see upgrade prompts when you try to access features above your tier:

| Feature | Where Upgrade Prompt Appears |
|---------|------------------------------|
| Raw Materials | Inventory → Raw Materials |
| Recipes | Recipes menu item |
| Receipt Printing | POS → After checkout |
| Cash Drawer | Settings → Cash Drawer |
| Profit Analytics | Reports → Profit Margins |
| Waste Tracking | Reports → Waste |

The prompt reads:
> *"[Feature name] is available on [Growth/Pro] plans. Upgrade to unlock [benefit]."*
>
> [Contact Us to Upgrade]

### Step 8: Grace Period (Subscription Expiry)

If your subscription expires (Growth or Pro tier), you do not immediately lose access.

#### Grace Period Behavior

| Time | What Happens |
|------|-------------|
| Day 0 | Subscription expires |
| Days 1-5 | **Grace period** — full access continues as normal |
| Day 6+ | **Auto-downgrade to Free tier** — Growth/Pro features become inaccessible |

#### During Grace Period (Days 1-5)

- All features continue to work normally
- You see a banner: *"Your subscription expired on [date]. You have [X] days remaining in your grace period. Contact us to renew."*
- You can still use raw materials, recipes, receipt printing, and all Growth/Pro features

#### After Grace Period (Day 6+)

- Your shop is automatically downgraded to Free tier
- **Your data is NOT deleted** — raw materials, recipes, and history are all preserved
- Features become inaccessible:
  - Raw Materials menu disappears
  - Recipes menu disappears
  - Receipt printing stops
  - Reports show limited data
- You continue using the POS with Free tier limits (50 products, 50 orders/day)
- To restore access: renew your subscription

#### Renewing After Downgrade

1. Contact Ko Htun and pay for the new subscription period
2. Your tier is reactivated
3. All your previous data (raw materials, recipes, history) becomes accessible again
4. No data was lost during the downgrade period

  - Step indicator (1 of 4, 2 of 4, etc.)

## Error Handling

### "Email already registered"

- **When it happens:** Trying to sign up with an email that already has an account
- **User sees:** *"An account with this email already exists. Please sign in instead."*
- **User should:** Use the "Sign In" page, or use "Forgot Password" if they cannot remember their password

### "Email not verified"

- **When it happens:** Trying to sign in before clicking the email verification link
- **User sees:** *"Please verify your email address before signing in. Check your inbox for the verification link."*
- **User should:** Check their email inbox (and spam folder) for the verification email

### "Account not approved"

- **When it happens:** Trying to sign in after verification but before platform admin approval
- **User sees:** The "Pending Approval" screen described above
- **User should:** Wait for the approval email. Contact support if it has been more than 2 business days

### "Account deactivated"

- **When it happens:** An admin has deactivated the user's account
- **User sees:** *"Your account has been deactivated. Please contact support for assistance."*
- **User should:** Contact the platform administrator to understand why and resolve the issue

### "Subscription expired — grace period ended"

- **When it happens:** Signing in after the 5-day grace period has ended
- **User sees:** A banner: *"Your subscription has expired. You are now on the Free tier. Contact us to renew and restore full access."*
- **User should:** Contact Ko Htun to renew their subscription

## Examples

### Example 1: New Shop Owner — Full Onboarding

Ma Thandar opens "Sunrise Tea House" in Yangon.

1. **Day 1, 10:00 AM:** She visits the CoffeeShop POS website and signs up with her shop email (sunrise.tea@gmail.com), sets a password, and enters "Sunrise Tea House" as her shop name.
2. **Day 1, 10:02 AM:** She checks her Gmail and clicks the verification link.
3. **Day 1, 10:03 AM:** She tries to sign in. Sees "Pending Approval" screen. She waits.
4. **Day 1, 3:00 PM:** Ko Htun reviews her signup, sees it is a legitimate tea shop, and approves it.
5. **Day 1, 3:05 PM:** Ma Thandar receives an email: "Your shop has been approved!"
6. **Day 1, 3:10 PM:** She signs in again. The guided tour starts.
7. **Day 1, 3:15 PM:** She fills in shop settings: address, phone, uploads her logo.
8. **Day 1, 3:25 PM:** She adds her first 5 products: Green Tea, Milk Tea, Coffee, Mohinga, Samosa.
9. **Day 1, 3:40 PM:** She goes to the POS and makes her first test sale.
10. **Day 2:** She adds her remaining 15 products and starts serving real customers.

### Example 2: Upgrading from Free to Growth

Ko Zaw has been using the Free tier for 2 months. His shop is getting busy and he wants receipt printing and inventory tracking.

1. He messages Ko Htun on Viber: "I want to upgrade to Growth for 3 months."
2. Ko Htun replies with the amount: 49,000 MMK × 3 = 147,000 MMK.
3. Ko Zaw transfers 147,000 MMK via KBZpay and screenshots the receipt.
4. He sends the screenshot to Ko Htun on Viber.
5. Ko Htun verifies the payment and updates Ko Zaw's subscription to Growth.
6. Ko Zaw receives an email: "Your subscription has been upgraded to Growth!"
7. He signs in and sees new menu items: Raw Materials, Recipes.
8. He sets up his ingredients and creates recipes for his top 10 products.
9. From now on, every sale automatically deducts ingredients from stock.

### Example 3: Grace Period Scenario

Ko Min's Growth subscription expires on June 1st.

- **June 1-5 (Grace Period):** He continues using all Growth features normally. A yellow banner reminds him to renew.
- **June 3:** He contacts Ko Htun and pays for another month.
- **June 3 (same day):** Ko Htun activates his subscription. Banner disappears. No interruption.
- **If he had not renewed by June 6:** His shop would downgrade to Free. His raw materials and recipes would still exist in the database but would not be accessible until he renewed.
