# User Interview — Coffee POS

- **Who:** A friend — owner of the coffee shop (real daily user)
- **When:** July 2026 (ongoing daily use, informal conversations)
- **How:** In-person, at the shop — recurring feedback over time

## What they do today (without your project)
- Tablet used as the POS checkout device; phone used by the owner to view reports
- After checkout, connects to a printer to issue a receipt for the customer

## What they liked
- Installed the PWA on their phone as owner — easy to check sales reports from anywhere
- Checkout flow on the tablet connects smoothly to the printer to issue receipts

## What confused them / what's missing
- (Previously) planned kitchen integration + food costing feature — owner found it too complex and asked to drop it; app is simpler now without it
- Some customers ask for receipts/vouchers, but there's no dedicated printer yet — only a test printer is being used

## What would make them actually use it more
- A dedicated shop printer (the owner is currently buying one with their own money, since this is a free app I'm building for them)

## What I'll change (next steps)
- [ ] Test the receipt/voucher printing flow once the dedicated printer arrives
- [ ] Keep kitchen/food-costing feature out of scope based on this feedback
